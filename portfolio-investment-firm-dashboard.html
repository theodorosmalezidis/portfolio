About This Project

This Tableau dashboard for an investment firm presents key performance indicators with year-over-year (YoY) changes, tracks quarterly trends in assets under management (AUM), highlights annual client retention rates, and compares branch performance across key metrics. It offers a clear, data-driven view of the firm’s growth, client loyalty, and operational efficiency.
